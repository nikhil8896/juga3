Frequently Asked Questions

What are the tournament rules?

+

All tournaments are played under the official WPBSA (Snooker) or WPA (Pool) rules, with specific local bylaws available in the tournament info pack.

What is the dress code?

+

For professional/open events, a waistcoat, collared shirt, and dress shoes are required. Amateur events have a smart casual dress code (no jeans, no trainers).

Can I register a team?

+

Currently, we only host individual competitions. We plan to introduce team events in the future.

What is the refund policy?

+

Registration fees are refundable up to 7 days before the tournament start date.

How are rankings calculated?

+

Rankings are based on a points system that considers tournament performance, opponent strength, and round reached. Points reset at the start of each season.
